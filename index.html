System Management Stock Frozen Food Bintang Jaya Group

Frozen Food Stock Management System This system is designed for Bintang Jaya Group partners, especially in the frozen food sector, to manage product stock efficiently. Background - The frozen food business requires accurate stock management so that the operational process runs smoothly. - Unmonitored product stock can cause shortages or overstock, which leads to losses. - A web-based system is needed to record incoming and outgoing goods transactions, and display real-time stock reports. Project Objectives 1. Automation of Stock Process: Manage incoming and outgoing goods data digitally. 2. Time Efficiency: Minimize manual processes in recording transactions. 3. Data Accuracy: Reduces input errors and redundancies. 4. Real-Time Reporting: Provides up-to-date stock reports to aid decision-making. Key Features 1. Product Management: - Add, delete, and edit product data. - Display product information such as name, stock, and price. 2. Incoming and Outgoing Transactions: - Record goods entering the warehouse. - Record items sold or out of the warehouse. 3. Admin Dashboard: - Summary of product stock. 4. System Login: - Secure access to the system with user authentication. Technology Used 1. Frontend: - HTML, CSS, 2. Backend: - PHP for business logic process. 3. Database: - MySQL for transaction and product data storage. System Advantages - Scalability: Can be integrated with additional features such as stock prediction. - Security: Data is protected with a login system and password hashing. - Accessibility: Web-based system that can be accessed anytime through a browser.
